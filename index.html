MaratonIME Spring School

The MaratonIME ICPC Spring School 2018 is organized by MaratonIME and hosted by IME-USP during one week from October 8th to October 12th. Our main goal is to train ICPC contestants from all over the world for the national regionals and the World Finals.

The instructor is from Slovakia, so all the activities will be held in English.

All interested students must register individually through the registration page. If you have a team, all three members must register individually. There is no fee, but we will not provide any kind of food, housing or transportation.

We are studying the possibility of live streaming the classes. Don't forget to like our facebook page for further information and photos.
